SongSwap

Help

This application allows you to convert your Spotify playlist into a YouTube playlist. To do so, head over to the home page where you will see a list of your Spotify playlists. Next to each playlist, you will see a green convert button. Pressing this button will start the conversion of the associated playlist. After converting the playlist, you will have the option to open the new YouTube playlist in a new tab and/or go back to the home page. If you wish to convert a playlist that isn't your own, you can click the white 'Convert A Different Playlist' button on the home page. After pressing this button, you will be met with a search bar where you will need to enter the playlist ID of the playlist you want to convert. To find the playlist ID, you can go to the playlist on Spotify and press 'Share'. You will then need to select the 'copy link' sharing option. The link that you copied will look something like: https://open.spotify.com/playlist/6OwabUkFXfXPeBmuucTF7I?si=95fc3f7d9d6540f4, however, the part that contains the ID is the part after the 'playlist/' That is, the '6OwabUkFXfXPeBmuucTF7I?si=95fc3f7d9d6540f4' part. This is the playlist ID. Once you enter this into the search bar, press the convert button and the same steps from before can now be followed. However, if the ID of the playlist you entered doesn't exist, you will be met with a page telling you so. If this is the case, you can either try to enter it again or go back to the home page.
